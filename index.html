Private speedboat charter
Full Day

Full Day

The best activity of the Split days and Jeremy's highlight of the whole trip. Spectacular travertine waterfalls connected by wooden boardwalks through forest. Swimming at the base of the falls in crystal-clear turquoise water. Boat from Skradin makes the arrival scenic and fun. Back via Trogir (UNESCO medieval island town, 30 min detour, worth it).

  • 17:00 — Back in Hvar

Montenegro — Kotor
Day trip from Sheraton
Day Trip

The most underrated day of the trip. Drive 1.5 hours to the Bay of Kotor — a UNESCO fjord-like bay ringed by dramatic mountains, one of the most beautiful landscapes in Europe. Kotor's medieval Old Town is smaller and less crowded than Dubrovnik. Delphine and the kids explore the city walls, Chris finds a waterfront café. Lunch in the old town, back at the Sheraton for a swim by 18:00.

📍 Montenegro
  • Drive from Sheraton ~1.5 hours (hire car or private driver)
  • Kotor Old Town — UNESCO walled city
  • City walls hike — Delphine + kids (Chris waits below)
  • Perast village + Our Lady of the Rocks island (en route)
  • Seafood lunch in Kotor old town
  • Back at Sheraton by 18:00
⚠️ Notes
  • Bring passports — you cross the border into Montenegro
  • No visa needed for EU citizens
  • Montenegro uses Euro (€)
  • Private driver makes it much easier than self-driving
  • Book driver through Sheraton concierge
